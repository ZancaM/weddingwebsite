Travel Information

Train Website: www.trenitalia.com

There are different choices for those flying to Italy the closest airports are Verona, Venice and Milan. The flights will have different prices depending on what airport you are leaving from and the one you are landing to. All Italian airports offer trains that connect to the city of Trento (Closest city to the wedding).

From Milan Malpensa it will take approximately 4 hrs with train. You will have to take a shuttle bus ( shuttle website link ) from the airport to Milano Centrale train station. Milano Centrale station offers trains to Trento.

From Venice airport it takes between 3 and 4 hours to get to Trento. You will have to take a bus from the airport to the train station. There a train will take you to Verona and from Verona another train will take you to Trento. See Directions

From Verona airport you will have to take bus 199 to get to the train station (about 15 min). From there a train will take you straight to Trento in about an hour. See directions
